About me

Hi! My name is Rodrigo Martín, I live in Spain, and my favourite thing about the 3D industry is game developing and vfx, along with Modelling.
I would enjoy being part of a team, because of the feeling when a project is finally done, and it turns out just right because each part did their best.

Not much else that I can say here, but what I can say, is that i have lots more to offer! For example, I am a scout, if you need a wood refuge in your office, or to survive for a whole week in the wild, I am your man!

In summary, my dream is to work in a studio as a VFX artist where I can feel at home with the rest of the team.
